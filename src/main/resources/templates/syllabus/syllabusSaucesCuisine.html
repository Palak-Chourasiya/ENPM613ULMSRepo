Course Description:

Unizon School will take you beyond just recipes and lists of ingredients. Instead, we will take you fundamentals of Sauces, discussing, and practicing all the fundamental techniques that will help student to make their own Sauces.

In this course you will learn all of the basic knowledge needed to truly become a confident with Sauces. This course will teach basic techniques and essential equipment to make various type of Sauces.

COURSE OBJECTIVES:

Upon successful completion of the course, the student will be able to know some of the key foods and ingredients and be able to make his/her own dishes.

MODULES

Module 1:   How to make Brown Sauces
Module 2:   How to make Tomato Sauces
Module 3:   How to make Hot Sauces
Module 4:   How to make Fish Sauces
Module 5:   How to make Butter Sauces
