Course Description:

Unizon School will take you beyond just recipes and lists of ingredients. Instead, we will take you fundamentals of Indian Cuisine.

In this course you will learn all of the basic knowledge needed to truly become a confident with Indian Dishes. This course will teach basic techniques and essential equipment to make various type of Indian Foods.

COURSE OBJECTIVES:

Upon completion of this course you will have learned and possess a thorough understanding of the Indian cuisine cooking tradition.
