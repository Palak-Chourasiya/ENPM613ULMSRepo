Course Description:

Unizon School will take you beyond just recipes and lists of ingredients. Instead, we will take you fundamentals of mediterranean Cuisisne, discussing, and practicing all the fundamental techniques that will help student to make their own dishes.

In this course you will learn all of the basic knowledge needed to truly become a confident with the mediterranean Cuisine. This course will teach basic techniques and essential equipment to make various type of mediterranean Foods.

COURSE OBJECTIVES:

Upon successful completion of the course, the student will be able to know some of the key foods and ingredients and be able to make his/her own dishes.
