Course Description:

Unizon School will take you beyond just recipes and lists of ingredients. Instead, we will take you fundamentals of baking, discussing, and practicing all the fundamental techniques that make us better bakers.

In this course you will learn all of the basic knowledge needed to truly become a confident baking. This course will teach basic baking techniques, essential equipment and techike to make various type of baking products.

COURSE OBJECTIVES:

Upon successful completion of the course, the student will be able to make various types of baking products

MODULES

Module 1:   How to make Muffins
Module 2:   How to make whipped Topping
Module 3:   How to make Cookies
Module 4:   How to make Brownies
Module 5:   How to make Glazes
