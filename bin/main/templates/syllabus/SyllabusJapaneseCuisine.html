Course Description:

Unizon School will take you beyond just recipes and lists of ingredients. Instead, we will take you fundamentals of Japanese Cuisisne, discussing, and practicing all the fundamental techniques that will help student to make their own dishes.

In this course you will learn all of the basic knowledge needed to truly become a confident with the Japanese Cuisine. This course will teach basic techniques and essential equipment to make various type of Japanee Foods.

COURSE OBJECTIVES:

Upon successful completion of the course, the student will be able to know some of the key foods and ingredients at a local Japanese (Asian) grocery store and and be able to make his/her own dishes.

MODULES

Module 1:   How to make Rice dishes
Module 2:   How to make side dishes
Module 3:   How to make Sweets and snacks
Module 4:   How to make Grilled Dishes
Module 5:   How to make Tea and other drinks
